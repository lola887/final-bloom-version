About US

Founder of Bloomers AI

Hi, I'm Alla Alanazi , a passionate advocate for emotional well-being and psychology. Combining my love for coding and emotional health, I created this platform to help people become more aware, conscious, and emotionally resilient. This website is my graduation project, but it’s also the start of a lifelong mission to support emotional healing and reach as many people as possible.

I’ve spent years reading and learning about healing, and I’m committed to sharing knowledge and tools that can make a real difference in people’s lives. In the future, this platform will grow to include more resources, tools, and community features.
